About Burger Snipe

Burger Snipe, founded in 2017 in the bustling town of Tanza, Cavite, Philippines, started with a mission to redefine the burger experience by delivering mouthwatering flavors crafted from the freshest ingredients. It quickly gained a loyal following for its innovative menu and dedication to quality, leading to an impressive expansion worldwide. By 2020, Burger Snipe cemented its place as the No. 1 burger shop in the world, celebrated for its signature creations that combined bold flavors with a touch of Filipino culinary heritage.
